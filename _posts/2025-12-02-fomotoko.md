An e-commerce platform designed for internet marketers to source products and manage sales through customizable order forms. The platform provides end-to-end e-commerce capabilities including product sourcing, checkout systems, payment processing, and customer support, helping internet marketers to operate and scale their online businesses quickly. As a Full-stack Developer, I architected and delivered most of the platform's core features while conducting hands-on user training.

## My Contributions

- **Third-Party Logistics Integration**: Integrated multiple 3PL providers for automated shipping requests, tracking, and fulfillment, enabling order delivery across Indonesia.
- **Product Management**: Built comprehensive product listing systems with variant management, pricing controls, and inventory synchronization for internet marketers (users).
- **Sub-Account Management**: Developed role-based sub-account system allowing internet marketers to add customer service admins with specific permissions to handle orders and support tickets.
- **Checkout System**: Developed a flexible checkout flow supporting multiple payment methods, address validation, order customization that fits the needs of internet marketers.
- **Payment Gateway Integration**: Integrated secure payment gateways to handle transactions, ensuring PCI compliance and data security.
- **Voucher & Promotions**: Implemented voucher systems with various discount types, usage limits, and campaign management features.
- **Customer Ticketing**: Created a support ticketing system for handling customer inquiries, complaints, and order issues with proper tracking and resolution workflows.
- **User Training**: Conducted hands-on training sessions for end-users, ensuring smooth platform adoption and effective feature utilization.
