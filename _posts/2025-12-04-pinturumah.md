## My Contributions

- **Property Listings**: Developed comprehensive property listing system showcasing available houses with detailed specifications, pricing, and location information.
- **Survey Request System**: Built functionality allowing potential buyers to schedule property surveys and site visits directly through the platform.
- **Construction Progress Tracking**: Implemented progress monitoring features displaying real-time updates on building development, complete with photos and milestone tracking to build customer trust.
- **Community Development Updates**: Created modules to showcase public infrastructure progress including parks, roads, and amenities, giving buyers full visibility into neighborhood development.
- **Stakeholder Collaboration**: Translated business requirements from property sales teams into technical specifications, ensuring the platform met real-world sales needs.
- **User Training**: Conducted hands-on training sessions for sales agents and admin staff, ensuring effective platform adoption and usage.
- **Full Project Lifecycle**: Managed end-to-end development from initial requirements gathering, technical implementation, deployment, to post-launch support.
